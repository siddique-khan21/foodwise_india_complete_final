Platform Rules & Guidelines

Ensuring safety, quality, and trust for all users

Donor Rules

  • Donate only safe, hygienic, and uncontaminated food.
  • Provide accurate details about food quantity, quality, and pickup time.
  • Ensure proper packaging before pickup (covered, spill-proof, veg/non-veg labelled).s
  • Be available at the scheduled pickup time or update changes in advance.
  • Do not demand money or compensation for donations.
  • Follow all local food safety and municipal guidelines.
  • Violation of these rules may lead to suspension, termination, or legal action.

NGO/Receiver Rules

  • OVerify food quality at pickup and report unsafe food immediately.
  • Collect donations within the committed time window.
  • Use donated food only for beneficiaries‚Äînot for resale
  • Maintain transparency and records when requested.
  • Violations may lead to suspension, removal from the platform, or legal action

Volunteer Rules

  • Only assist approved pickup requests
  • Arrive on time for pickups and follow assigned routes.
  • Respect donor and receiver privacy
  • Do not open, consume, or tamper with donated food.
  • Upload proof (photos, delivery confirmation) when required.
  • Use the app responsibly‚Äîno fake updates or location misuse.
  • Any violation may lead to suspension, blacklisting, or legal action.

Compost/Animal Feed Farm Rules

  • Accept only food waste suitable for composting or animal feed.
  • Ensure eco-friendly disposal and follow environmental regulations.
  • Do not mix plastic or non-biodegradable items with collected waste.
  • Follow all local agricultural and livestock safety guidelines.
  • Coordinate pickup schedules efficiently
  • Violations may lead to termination or legal environmental action.
